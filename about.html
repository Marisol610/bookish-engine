Marisol Rodriguez Biography

My name is Marisol Rodriguez. I was born in Bronx New york. I recieved the Associates Degree in Computer Programming in 1993 from EDP College of Puerto Rico. During this same period i was employed as a Forecast Analist and Planner for Newell Rubbermaid Co. After earning my degree I was able to work for the company for ten years. Several years later my husband and I moved to Milwaukee, WI where we finished raising our children. In the year 2005 I was given the opportunity to go back to school and inspired to have my own baking business I enrolled at Milwakaukee Technical College for the Baking Program and eanrd my certificate in 2006. Immediately after graduation I opened my business. Later I became and Instructor at Micheal's Craft Stores and also for the Milwaukee Public Schools after school programs. Tired of the snow and the cold freezing weather. In 2015 my husband and I decided to move to Florida. Due to physical injuries from a car accident I was forced to stop working, and in 2017 I joined the Macy's team as a Visual Manager until this present time. I am also the founder of Generation 080 Ministry in, Bartow, Florida.
